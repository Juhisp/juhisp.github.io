About Me

I'm a doctoral researcher in the Inverse Problems group at the Department of Mathematics and Systems Analysis at Aalto University. My main research interests are in Bayesian optimal design methods and their applications in inverse problems and imaging.

Outside of work, I enjoy sports and exploration, and especially the combination of these: sailing, hiking, and ski touring.

I have also previously been active in Aaltoes, the student entrepreurship society in Aalto University, and was a Board Member in the Aaltoes alumni founded initiative Tuolinkantajat Ventures.
